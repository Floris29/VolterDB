Volter is a bot made by Floris and Leon. At first this project was for a client, but how further we came into the project, the client didn't do anything with it. So we decided to make it just our own Discord bot.

Our support server is every day open! You can just come in, and ask your questions or give your feedback!

5With what is the bot build?

Yes! You can find them on https://github.com/Floris29/Volter
